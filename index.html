Portumna, Co. Galway Lough Derg Rent-a-Bike is a bicycle rental shop based in Portumna, Co. Galway. Opening a year ago, we are a business which provides an excellent range of bicycles to rent. Our highly experienced team here at Lough Derg Rent-a-Bike also offer a professional bicycle repair and bicycle maintenance service to our customers. Want to explore Portumna and the surrounding Lough Derg which is the third largest lake in Ireland? Then visit Lough Derg Rent-a-Bike and do what it says on the shop name and rent a bike to traverse the beautiful scenery! Visit the Lake, Forest, Marina, Abbey and Castle in this scenic town. Portumna also sits on the River Shannon which divides the counties of Tipperary and Galway 2 KM from the town. Cycling is great for your physical and mental health and travelling around this beautiful lake is a way to fulfil these needs.
